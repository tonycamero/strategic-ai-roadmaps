# Constraint Intelligence Engine

The Constraint Intelligence Engine is the strategic reasoning core of StrategicAI.

It transforms operational signals and exception patterns into structural
diagnostics and strategic interventions.

Where the Operational Intelligence Engine detects *what is happening*, the
Constraint Intelligence Engine determines *why it is happening* and *what
should change*.

The engine continuously analyzes operational data produced by the
Operational Digital Twin and SAR Shield to identify structural constraints
within the organization.

---

# Purpose

Organizations often treat operational problems as isolated incidents.

Examples include:

inventory discrepancies  
production delays  
supply shortages  
slow customer response  

However, these issues frequently originate from deeper structural constraints.

The Constraint Intelligence Engine identifies these structural constraints
and converts them into actionable strategic proposals.

# Constraint Detection

The engine identifies recurring patterns that indicate structural constraints.

Examples include:

inventory variance concentrated in specific SKUs  
repeated production schedule failures  
persistent supply chain delays  
high exception escalation rates  

Detected constraints are represented as:

diagnostic_findings

Each finding includes:

constraint_type  
affected_operations  
evidence_signals  
severity_score  

---

# Diagnostic Runs

Constraint analysis is performed through structured diagnostic runs.

Diagnostic run lifecycle:

diagnostic_run_initialized  
signals_loaded  
pattern_analysis_executed  
constraints_detected  
diagnostic_report_generated  

Diagnostic runs produce a set of canonical findings describing structural
constraints within the organization.

---

# Relationship to Assisted Synthesis

Diagnostic findings feed into the Assisted Synthesis System (SAS).

SAS generates strategic proposals designed to resolve identified constraints.

Example flow:

diagnostic_finding
    ↓
SAS run initialized
    ↓
AI proposals generated
    ↓
Human elections
    ↓
selected proposals

These proposals represent potential structural interventions.

---

# Proposal Compilation

Elected proposals are compiled into a deterministic execution plan through
the Selection Envelope system.

Process:

selected proposals
    ↓
Selection Envelope compiled
    ↓
execution plan generated

The envelope defines:

required integrations  
required operational changes  
expected outcomes
