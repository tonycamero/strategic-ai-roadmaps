# Strategic AI Platform — Revenue Tiers & Packaging (Internal Only)

## Purpose

Define structured, tiered pricing ranges aligned to Intelligence Depth Layers.

This is an internal strategy document.
Not for public marketing use.

Pricing reflects:
- Data layer activation
- Intelligence depth
- Agent capability
- Organizational modeling complexity
- Support intensity

---

# Intelligence-Aligned Tier Structure

## Tier 0 — Strategic Diagnostic (Roadmap Only)

Positioning:
Front-end strategic clarity engagement.

Data Layers Activated:
Layer 1 — Strategic Signal Only

Includes:
- Strategic intake (Owner + select team)
- KPI baseline capture
- Org Vector classification
- Company Diagnostic Map
- AI Leverage Map
- 30/60-Day Roadmap

Trust Console Access:
- Roadmap Mode only
- No structural modeling
- No live data ingestion

Delivery Model:
One-time engagement (4–6 weeks)

Pricing Range:
$5,000 – $12,500 (based on org size & complexity)

Margin Target:
High (consulting-weighted)

Strategic Purpose:
Acquisition engine into subscription tiers.

---

## Tier 1 — Operational Intelligence (Core SaaS)

Positioning:
Structural visibility & fragility modeling.

Data Layers Activated:
Layer 1 + Layer 2

Unlocks:
- Organizational graph modeling
- Decision authority mapping
- System topology mapping
- Automation registry
- Control layer detection
- Role-adaptive Trust Console (structural mode)

Trust Console Capability:
- Structural modeling
- Missing control layer detection
- Failure cascade simulation (assumption-reduced)
- Configuration suggestions (non-executing)

Excludes:
- Live behavioral telemetry
- Predictive anomaly detection

Pricing Range (Monthly):
$1,500 – $4,000

Sizing Drivers:
- Team size
- System complexity
- Number of integrations mapped (even if not activated)

Annual Contract Equivalent:
$18,000 – $48,000

Strategic Purpose:
Shift client from consulting to recurring intelligence layer.

---

## Tier 2 — Live Intelligence (Integrated)

Positioning:
Real-time organizational intelligence & early warning system.

Data Layers Activated:
Layer 1 + Layer 2 + Layer 3

Unlocks:
- API integrations (CRM, POS, scheduling, ERP, etc.)
- Time-series performance modeling
- Perception vs reality comparison
- Anomaly detection flags
- KPI trend forecasting
- Volume stress simulation using live data

Trust Console Capability:
- Evidence-based modeling
- Predictive risk alerts
- Performance decay detection
- Constraint drift detection

Pricing Range (Monthly):
$4,000 – $10,000

Sizing Drivers:
- Revenue band
- Integration depth
- Data volume
- Multi-location complexity

Annual Contract Equivalent:
$48,000 – $120,000

Strategic Purpose:
Transition from structural advisory to live intelligence infrastructure.

---

## Tier 3 — Governance Intelligence (Enterprise / Advanced)

Positioning:
AI Governance & Strategic Risk Modeling Layer.

Data Layers Activated:
Layer 1–4 (Full Stack)

Unlocks:
- Governance profile registry
- Decision taxonomy engine
- Automation boundary modeling
- Escalation simulation
- Risk tolerance alignment modeling
- Compliance-aware advisory

Trust Console Capability:
- Governance-aware advisory
- Boundary-sensitive configuration warnings
- Irreversible decision classification
- Escalation requirement modeling

Pricing Range (Monthly):
$10,000 – $25,000+

Contract Model:
Enterprise agreement (12–24 months)
Custom scoping required.

Strategic Purpose:
Position platform as AI Infrastructure for complex organizations.

---

# Cross-Tier Revenue Strategy

Roadmap (Tier 0) → Tier 1 entry
Tier 1 → Tier 2 expansion via integrations
Tier 2 → Tier 3 governance upgrade

Intelligence depth increases with:
- Trust
- Data integration
- Executive buy-in
- Budget authority

---

# Internal Margin Considerations

Tier 0:
High human labor, high margin, no ongoing obligation.

Tier 1:
Moderate onboarding effort, strong recurring margin.

Tier 2:
Integration cost increases but defensibility increases significantly.

Tier 3:
Low churn risk, high enterprise defensibility.

# Strategic Positioning Notes

We are not selling automation tools.
We are selling Organizational Intelligence Depth.

Pricing must reflect:
- Risk visibility gained
- Structural fragility reduced
- Decision clarity increased
- Failure probability lowered

The deeper the intelligence layer, the higher the contract value.
