## Badge Definitions (For UI or Profile Page)

- **Foundation Builder** – Completed Owner Intake + Business Profile.
- **Team Assembler** – Invited at least 2 team members.
- **Full Team Activated** – All 3 team roles completed their intakes.
- **Diagnostic Ready** – Owner + profile complete and diagnostic generated.
- **Roadmap Owner** – Roadmap viewed end-to-end at least once.
- **Implementation Ready** – Ticket moderation completed.
- **Pilot Candidate** – Chosen an implementation path or requested a proposal.
