## 9Ô∏è‚É£ Risk Mitigation

### Outbound Risks

**Risk:** Low response rates  
**Mitigation:** A/B test email subject lines, try different messaging angles, increase LinkedIn touchpoints

**Risk:** Research Calls don't convert to Roadmaps

**Risk:** Roadmaps don't convert to Pilots  
**Mitigation:** Stronger value demonstration, show platform mockups, create urgency with cohort scarcity

### Delivery Risks

**Risk:** Platform MVP not ready for January  
**Mitigation:** Define absolute minimum viable scope, have manual backup plan (Google Sheets + ChatGPT access), communicate clearly with clients about "beta" status

**Risk:** Can't deliver 10 Pilots simultaneously  
**Mitigation:** Stagger start dates (2-3 per week), standardize setup process, create detailed runbooks

**Risk:** Clients don't adopt platform  
**Mitigation:** Mandatory kick-off training, weekly check-ins first month, gamify usage, tie success to their visible metrics
